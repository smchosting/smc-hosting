The Evolution of Excellence

Our Storied Legacy

Established 17 years ago, Sri Mulam Club has become a cornerstone of the Thiruvananthapuram community, offering a premier venue for a variety of events. Named after the revered Sree Mulam Thirunal Maharaja, the club was founded to create a space where cultural, corporate, and social gatherings could thrive in a sophisticated environment. Over the years, the club has evolved to meet the changing needs of event organizers and attendees, continually upgrading its facilities and services.Situated in Vazhuthacaud, near the iconic Tagore Theatre, the club's strategic location has played a significant role in its success. Accessibility and convenience have always been a priority, ensuring guests can easily reach the venue and enjoy a comfortable experience. Today, Sri Mulam Club continues to uphold its legacy of providing exceptional service and facilities, making it the go-to venue for memorable events in Thiruvananthapuram.

Our facilities are designed to cater to a wide range of events, including state-of-the-art auditoriums, elegant banquet halls, versatile community halls, and a fully equipped gym. Whether you are planning a corporate seminar, a cultural performance, a wedding, or a private celebration, our dedicated team is committed to making your event a success. We provide exceptional service from start to finish, with meticulous event planning and coordination, advanced audio-visual systems, and customizable seating arrangements. Accessibility and convenience are at the core of our values. Our venue features wheelchair-accessible facilities, ample parking space, and high-speed Wi-Fi to ensure all guests have a comfortable and connected experience. Our professional staff is always on hand to assist with any requirements, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable event for all guests.
