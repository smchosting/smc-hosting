GYM

Our state-of-the-art gym at Sri Mulam Club is designed to cater to all your fitness needs. Equipped with the latest exercise machines and free weights, it offers a comprehensive workout experience. Our professional trainers are available to provide personalized fitness plans and guidance. The gym is spacious and well-maintained, ensuring a comfortable and motivating environment. Whether you're looking to build strength, improve your cardio, or maintain overall fitness, our gym has everything you need to achieve your goals.

Tennis Court

Enjoy a game of tennis on our professionally maintained court at Sri Mulam Club. The court features high-quality surfaces and is designed to meet international standards, providing an excellent playing experience for all skill levels. Our facilities are open to members and guests, offering a great opportunity for both casual play and competitive matches. Equipment rental and professional coaching sessions are available for those looking to improve their game. Experience the thrill of tennis in a welcoming and well-equipped environment.

Shuttle Court

Our shuttle court at Sri Mulam Club is perfect for badminton enthusiasts of all levels. Featuring a well-maintained playing surface and excellent lighting, it ensures a top-notch experience for players. The court is available to members and guests, providing a great space for both casual games and competitive matches. We offer equipment rental and professional coaching sessions for those looking to enhance their skills. Come enjoy a game of badminton in a friendly and professional environment.

Bar

Relax and unwind at our elegant bar at Sri Mulam Club, offering a wide selection of premium beverages and expertly crafted cocktails. The sophisticated ambiance provides the perfect setting for socializing with friends or enjoying a quiet drink. Our skilled bartenders are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and creating memorable experiences. The bar also features a curated menu of delicious appetizers and snacks to complement your drink. Experience the perfect blend of luxury and comfort at our exclusive bar.

Restaurant

Indulge in a culinary delight at our restaurant at Sri Mulam Club, where gourmet dishes and a welcoming atmosphere await you. Our menu features a diverse selection of cuisines, prepared with the finest ingredients by our expert chefs. Whether you're dining with family, friends, or colleagues, our restaurant offers a perfect setting for any occasion. Enjoy impeccable service and a relaxing ambiance as you savor every bite. Experience the perfect blend of taste and elegance at our premier dining destination.

Community Hall

Our community halls at Sri Mulam Club provide the perfect venue for gatherings of all sizes. Spacious and versatile, these halls can be customized to host a variety of events, from meetings and workshops to social celebrations. Equipped with modern amenities and audio-visual systems, they ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience. Our dedicated staff is on hand to assist with planning and setup, ensuring your event runs smoothly. Discover the ideal setting for your next community event at our welcoming and well-equipped halls.

What We Provide

1000+ Seating Capacity
Our venue offers flexible seating arrangements to accommodate up to 1000 guests, ensuring comfort and convenience for all event sizes. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large conference, we have the space to suit your needs.
Ample Parking Space

Green Rooms
Our well-equipped green rooms and dressing areas provide performers, speakers, and bride and groom comfort and privacy. These spaces help you prepare and relax before taking the stage. Enjoy a professional and accommodating environment.
